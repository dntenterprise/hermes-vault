# PupRoam Email Templates

Complete email template system for PupRoam. Covers all customer touchpoints from signup to post-purchase.

## Templates

### 1. Welcome Email
Trigger: New subscriber. Brand intro, product tease, call to follow socials.

### 2. Order Confirmation
Trigger: Order placed. Order details, delivery estimate (9-18 days from China), proactively manages expectations.

### 3. Shipping Confirmation
Trigger: Tracking available. Tracking URL, transit info, manages China→EU timeline.

### 4. Delivery Confirmation
Trigger: Delivered. Celebration email, social media CTA, opens feedback channel.

### 5. Follow-up / Review Request
Trigger: 7 days post-delivery. Review ask, emphasizes small team + customer feedback.

### 6. Abandoned Cart
Trigger: 1 hour after cart abandonment. Gentle reminder, 3 bullet features, direct cart link.

## Brand Voice
- Warm, adventurous, personal
- Swedish/English mix
- Short sentences
