## 🏆 Top 5 Free Tools to Start Dropshipping in 2026

### #1 — DSers (Free Forever)
AliExpress dropshipping fulfillment — 3,000 products, 3 stores, unlimited orders, no time limit. Official AliExpress partner. Works with Shopify, WooCommerce, Wix.

### #2 — Square Online (Free Plan)
Full e-commerce website with unlimited products, $0/month. SSL, SEO, social selling. Pay only transaction fees when you sell.

### #3 — Google Trends + TikTok Creative Center + Amazon BSR (Free)
The $0 product research trifecta. Filter Google Trends by Sweden/Europe. Check TikTok Creative Center for Scandinavian trending content. Cross-reference with Amazon DE bestsellers.

### #4 — Claude + Gemini (Free Tiers)
Claude Sonnet 4 (~25 msg/5h) for quality copy + Gemini 2.5 Flash for research and Swedish translations.

### #5 — TikTok Organic + Pinterest SEO (Free)
Zero-cost traffic generation. TikTok popular in Sweden (18-35), Pinterest strong in Nordics.

## Recommended $0 Startup Path

```
Week 1-2: Research
  - Google Trends + TikTok Creative Center → identify 3 niches
  - Amazon DE Bestsellers → validate demand in EU
  - Claude free → write product descriptions in English + Swedish

Week 3-4: Build
  - Square Online (free) → set up store with 10-20 products
  - Leonardo AI (free tokens) → generate product lifestyle images
  - DSers (free) → source products from AliExpress

Week 5+: Market
  - TikTok Organic → 3 posts/day, product demos
  - Pinterest SEO → 10-30 pins/day, keyword-rich
  - Google Merchant Center → submit free product listings
  - CJ Dropshipping → switch to EU warehouse (7-15 day shipping) for winning products

Cost after 30 days: $0 in tools
Cost after first sale: Transaction fees only (2.9% + $0.30 on Square)
```
