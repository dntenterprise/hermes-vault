# PupRoam — Marketing Plan

**Client:** PupRoam (puproam.store)
**Date:** 2026-06-06 (Lördag)
**Stage:** Pre-seed / bootstrapped
**Plan horizon:** 12 months
**Format:** AARRR (Acquisition → Activation → Retention → Referral → Revenue)

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**3 big bets:**
1. **TikTok/Reels content virality** — the spherical reflow mechanism is inherently visual. One satisfying demo video is the highest-leverage move for $0 budget.
2. **Micro-influencer gifting** — 20–30 Swedish micro-influencers (dogs, travel, outdoor) get free bottles. User-generated content feeds everything else.
3. **Shopify organic SEO** — long-tail pet travel keywords + product page optimization. Compounding returns over 6–12 months.

**90-day priorities:**
- Week 1–2: Product photos (user provides) → upload + optimize product page
- Week 3–4: TikTok/IG account setup + first 10 organic posts
- Week 5–8: Identify + contact 20 micro-influencers for gifting
- Week 9–12: First influencer content lands → repost + iterate

**12-month outcome:** 200–500 orders total (blended Sweden + EU), with organic growth from content + influencer UGC. No paid ads until revenue confirms product-market fit.

---

## 2. Strategic Frame

**Category claim:** *The only spherical pet water bottle that reflows. No squeeze. No mess. Just drink.*

**ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):**
- **Primary:** Urban dog owners, 25–40, Sweden/Europe, active lifestyle, Instagram/TikTok active, care about design + sustainability
- **Secondary:** Outdoor/travel enthusiasts who bring their dog on hikes, camping, road trips
- **Tertiary:** Gift buyers (dog-owning friends/family)

**Business model logic:**
- D2C via Shopify
- Single SKU launch → expand to variants (colors, sizes) after validation
- Margin from China sourcing (CJ), plan DE warehouse for faster EU shipping when volume justifies

**Brand voice non-negotiables:**
- Short, punchy, confident — like a product description, not a novel
- "This pup was born to roam" — adventure tone, not luxury
- English-first (product page, social), Swedish for Sweden market where relevant
- No corporate speak. No fake enthusiasm. Speak like a founder who loves dogs.

## 5. Activation (Aware → First Purchase)

### Current funnel

```
Shop visit → Product page → Add to cart → Checkout → Payment → Order
```

### What's missing

- **Social proof:** Zero reviews, zero testimonials. This is the #1 conversion blocker.
- **Trust signals:** Trust bar exists (4.9★, 850+, 30 days, free delivery) but numbers are fictional until real orders come in.
- **Product photos:** No real images of the bottle in use. This kills conversion.
- **Urgency:** No scarcity or time-limited offers.

### 90-day activation moves

1. **Product photos (Weeks 1–2):** User provides real photos of the bottle. Upload to product page. At minimum: hero shot, size reference (next to a phone/water bottle), action shot (dog drinking), lifestyle shot (in a backpack/hiking).
2. **First order validation (Weeks 3–4):** Run a soft launch to friends/family. Get 5–10 real orders → capture real reviews.
3. **Review collection (ongoing):** Post-purchase email asking for review + photo. Shopify product reviews app (free).
4. **Cart abandonment email (Weeks 5–6):** Set up 3-email abandonment flow in Brevo (free tier: 300 emails/day).

### Activation metrics

| Metric | Target (90 days) |
|---|---|
| Product page view → add to cart | ≥5% |
| Add to cart → purchase | ≥20% |
| Bounce rate | <60% |
| Average session duration | >90s |

## 6. Retention (Buy → Repeat)

### Reality check for single-SKU hardware

Pet water bottles are not a consumable. Customers may buy ONE and never return. Retention strategy must focus on:
1. **Accessories** — different lid colors, spare silicone bowls, carrying pouches (future SKUs)
2. **Gifting** — bottle makes a great gift for other dog owners
3. **Multi-pack** — "one for the car, one for the hiking bag"
4. **Mailing list** — content that keeps the brand top-of-mind

### 90-day retention moves

1. **Post-purchase email sequence (3 emails):**
   - Day 0: "Your PupRoam is on its way! 🐾" (order confirmation + unboxing tips)
   - Day 7: "How's your pup enjoying the roam?" (ask for review + photo)
   - Day 21: "Born to roam — adventure ideas for you and your dog" (blog posts, tips, UGC roundup)

2. **Brevo list building:** Add "Subscribe for adventure tips" popup to store (Shopify free popup or similar).

3. **Loyalty program research (Q3):** Evaluate Shopify free loyalty apps (Smile.io free tier).

### Retention metrics

| Metric | Target (6 months) |
|---|---|
| Repeat purchase rate | ≥10% (after 2+ SKUs) |
| Email CTR | ≥3% |
| Review rate | ≥5% of orders |
| Unsubscribe rate | <1% |

## 7. Referral (Customers → Bring More)

### Strategy: Gifting as the primary referral mechanic

A physical product that delights has natural word-of-mouth. Amplify it:

1. **Influencer gifting** (Weeks 5–8): 20 bottles sent to micro-influencers. Each post = referral source. Track via unique discount code per influencer.
2. **Friends/family soft launch** (Weeks 3–4): First 10 orders come from personal network. Ask them to share on social.
3. **Referral discount (Month 3+):** "Give €5, get €5" — Shopify referral apps (free tiers exist).

### 90-day referral moves

1. Create influencer discount codes: `PUPPACKLEIA`, `PUPPACKBARK`, etc.
2. Add social sharing button to order confirmation page
3. Print "Share PupRoam with a friend" card in each shipment (user prints at home — 10 cards, no cost)
